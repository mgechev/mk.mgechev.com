How to play
Player 1 Player 2
  • G - left, J - right, H - down, Y up
  • Press "A" for high-punch
  • Press "S" for low-punch
  • Press "D" for low-kick
  • Press "F" for high-kick
  • Press "Shift" for block
  • Use arrows to move
  • Press "P" for high-punch
  • Press "[" for low-punch
  • Press "]" for low-kick
  • Press "\" for high-kick
  • Press "Ctrl" for block
Press "Space" for restart
